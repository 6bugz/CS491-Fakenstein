Our project is about Deepfake, which combines deep learning and fake keywords. In summary, Deepfake replaces a person's features with someone else's. In our project, we will be targeting photos. We will try to replace people in the background with Deepfake's randomly generated outputs.

What is our project?

Our project idea will provide the users with a mobile application that will place a computer-generated face on the faces of the people in the background of photos. This product will both protect the privacy of the people in the background and will preserve the natural look of the photos since these people will not need to be removed from the images which might cause the photo to look edited and unnatural.
